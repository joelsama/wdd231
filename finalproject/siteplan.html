Site Purpose

This website will serve as a centralized platform for the tech ecosystem in Lagos. It will provide information about meetups, tech news, job boards, learning resources, and membership for community events.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find upcoming tech events or meetups happening in Lagos?
  • How do I join the Lagos Tech Community and get involved in local projects?
  • Which startups or companies are currently hiring developers in Lagos?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #004d99 (used for headers, nav background)
  • Accent Color: #ff9800 (used for buttons, highlights)
  • Background: #f4f4f4
  • Text: #333333

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto Slab', serif
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
  • Headings will use Roboto Slab for a strong, tech-friendly look. Paragraphs and body content will use Open Sans for readability.
